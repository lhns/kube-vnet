kube-vnet is installed.

The VirtualNetwork CRD is registered. Define a network and label pods to join it:

  kubectl get vnet -A
  kubectl get networkpolicy -A -l kube-vnet/managed-by=kube-vnet

Sample manifests are in the project repo at
https://github.com/lhns/kube-vnet/tree/main/config/samples

WARNING: ingressIsolation.mode is "pod". Every namespace not in
disabledNamespaces and not in ingressIsolation.namespaceOverrides.none will
get the default-deny baseline (no ingress allowed except via vnet membership
policies). Workloads that previously relied on default-allow will lose
ingress connectivity from outside their vnet.

By default, kube-system / kube-public / kube-node-lease are protected via
ingressIsolation.namespaceOverrides.none. Keep them there unless you have a
deliberate reason to apply a baseline to the control plane.
